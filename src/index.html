HoMM3 Explorer

A browser-based file explorer for Heroes of Might & Magic III archive formats. Browse, preview, and export assets — images, animations, sounds, fonts, videos, maps, RMG templates, and savegames — directly from LOD, PAK, SND, VID, DEF, PCX, D32, FNT, H3M, H3C, H3T, GM1/GM2/TGM/CGM savegames, EXE (GOG/Inno Setup, InstallShield), ISO disc images, SIT (StuffIt) archives, and Mac VISE 3.6 installers without installing anything.
